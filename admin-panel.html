⏰ Delivery Slot Management

Enable/disable slots based on order load. Slots are automatically disabled 1 hours before their start time.

📌 Auto-disable Rule

Any time slot will be automatically disabled 1 hours before its scheduled start time. For example:

  • 12 PM - 4 PM slot: Disabled after 10 AM
  • 4 PM - 8 PM slot: Disabled after 2 PM
  • 8 PM - 10 PM slot: Disabled after 6 PM
